 At Blockspector, we understand the importance of trust and transparency when it comes to managing your finances. Unfortunately, the world of web3 and the crypto community has recently been plagued by multiple scandals and a trend of companies' incapability of securing funds. This has unfortunately led to millions of retail investors dealing with the consequences.
 We've seen this firsthand with companies like FTX, Luna, and 3AC, which have become notorious for creating a stereotype of negative aspects within this space.
 This fundamentally has to change if we want people to feel confident about entrusting their savings within the decentralised finance space.
